Welcome to Shaggies

At Shaggies, we believe in offering more than simple dog haircuts. Our talented
grooming team is committed to providing an experience that leaves your dog 
feeling rejuvenated and looking great. Our services are customized to meet 
your specific demands or requirements, whether you prefer current, new,
trendy or traditional cuts for your doggy. With an emphasis on sensitive care
and attention to detail, we guarantee a positive and soothing experience for
your dog. Whether it's a simple bath or a complete grooming session, Shaggies
is dedicated to providing outstanding service and making each dog shine. From
stylish, fashionable jerseys to healthy, delicious treats to gentle and vibrant
shampoos to connect with your doggy and bond over a wash at home...
